### p11_video_render (V8.6 Step 10 + Step 11 video half + D-36-08 parallel dispatch)
- **EXPERT**: visual_executor (animator sub-step) + audio_pipeline (lip_sync sub-step) — atomic per shot, fan-out across shots
- **INPUT_SLOTS**: ["shot-list", "scene-images", "character-assets", "voice-timeline"]
- **OUTPUT_SLOTS**: ["video-clips", "lip-sync-reports"]
- **GATE_ID**: "render-preview"  (Gate 7 per V8.6 — operator confirms video clips meet quality bar)
- **parallel_shots**: int = 4 kwarg on run() (D-36-08 — p11 ONLY)
- **Implementation**: For each shot in shot-list, submit a delegate_task call to ThreadPoolExecutor(max_workers=parallel_shots). Aggregate results. Write aggregated video-clips + lip-sync-reports.
- **Goal shape (per shot)**: "Apply the visual_executor (animator) AND audio_pipeline (lip_sync) expert skills to render shot <N>... skill_view for both... produce video clip + lip-sync report... emit JSON shaped as {video_clip: {...}, lip_sync_report: {...}}"
- **Tests**: use `parallel_shots=1` OR monkeypatch ThreadPoolExecutor with a FakePool that runs sequentially. Assert that with parallel_shots=4 and 4 shots, delegate_task is called exactly 4 times (once per shot).
</phase_specs>
